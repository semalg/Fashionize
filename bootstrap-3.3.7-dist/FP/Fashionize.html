Our Mission:


In a rising technological world, online shopping is becoming more prevalent. Clothes shopping, when you can’t physically see or try on the product, is even more difficult. That’s why we at Fashionize created this site. Our goal is to create a medium where girls and women of any body type can come and discover their sizes and use those for a more accurate fit, on or offline!

About Us

Semal:

Semal is a former astronaut who has gone to the moon and back three times. After having lived in a space suit for a total of 29 years, she has made it a goal to develop the most fashionable space suit in the world. She plans, after accomplishing such goal, to create a line of space suits so people can have many options while in space to change into.

Madeline:

Madeline is interested in space and interned at NASA the summer before her junior year of college. She is currently in college majoring in astronomy and minoring in computer science. She enjoys fashion greatly and living in New York is always inspiring her to create her own style.

Melissa:

Melissa was raised in Los Angeles, she’s a big city girl. One day walked into Dash, store owned by the Kardashians, she fell in love with it. From that day forward, she's wanted to be her own boss and create a store better than Dash. She plans on making her store in New York within the next year.

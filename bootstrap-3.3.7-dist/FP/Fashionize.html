Our Mission:


In a rising technological world, online shopping is becoming more prevalent. Clothes shopping, when you can’t physically see or try on the product, is even more difficult. That’s why we at Fashionize created this site. Our goal is to create a medium where girls and women of any body type can come and discover their sizes and use those for a more accurate fit, on or offline!
